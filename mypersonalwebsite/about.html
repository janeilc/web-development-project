About

My name is Janeil Chambers and I am an Honors Information Technology student at Kennesaw State University. I keen on learning about the Cybersecurity field and on learning to code. One of the main reasons why I wanted to further my education is because I believe that more women should take an interest in the STEM field. STEM is a highly male-dominated industry, so the perspectives of research and ideas are partial. Introducing women to these fields can result in greater diversity in not only perspectives but also the workforce. I aim to use my degree to inspire other women to pursue careers in STEM disciplines and have a good impact on my community.
